# AI or Not?

An open-source game for deepfake literacy and AI education. Players view images and videos and guess whether they are AI-generated or real.

Built for Scouting America AI Merit Badge (Requirements 2d, 5) and general youth education.

## Play

Visit [brockwebb.github.io/ai-or-not](https://brockwebb.github.io/ai-or-not/) to play.

## Status

Under active development.
